Start here

Today, do only this

  1. Read the How to page. Learn the weekly rhythm and choose spiritual, practical or combined use.
  2. Look briefly at both parts. Open the Course and the Practical course so you know what each contains.
  3. Prepare one ordinary notebook and pen. Do not buy or organise anything elaborate.
  4. Then open Week 1. Read Sunday’s orientation and stop when it tells you to stop.

This page exists to prevent an enthusiastic first day from becoming an exhausting redesign of life.
