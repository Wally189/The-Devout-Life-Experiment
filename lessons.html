The course

St Francis de Sales is read in smaller portions. Each week begins on Sunday, asks for roughly 20–30 minutes a day, and ends with a short handwritten review. Each week has its own questions and amendment.

Choose your pace. Follow the spiritual course alone, use the Practical course alone, or bring them together through one amendment. Repeat, pause or slow a week where needed.

Do not restart. Resume from the next session or repeat the current week.
