Prayers for the work

Use the prayer suited to the moment. The aim is not to accumulate devotions, but to return gently and faithfully to God. Established prayers and original course prayers are identified separately.

Before a spiritual lesson

Course prayer

Original to this course

Lord Jesus Christ, give me light to see what is true, humility to accept it, courage to amend what is wrong, and peace to begin again when I fall. Let this work lead not to self-absorption but to love of You and faithful service of others. Amen.

Before practical work

Come, Holy Spirit

Established Catholic prayer

Come, Holy Spirit, fill the hearts of your faithful and kindle in them the fire of your love. Send forth your Spirit and they shall be created, and you shall renew the face of the earth.

O God, who by the light of the Holy Spirit did instruct the hearts of the faithful, grant that by the same Holy Spirit we may be truly wise and ever rejoice in his consolations. Through Christ our Lord. Amen.

Beginning the day

Morning Offering

A common Catholic form

O Jesus, through the Immaculate Heart of Mary, I offer you my prayers, works, joys and sufferings of this day for all the intentions of your Sacred Heart, in union with the Holy Sacrifice of the Mass throughout the world, for the salvation of souls, the reparation of sins, the reunion of all Christians, and in particular for the intentions of the Holy Father this month. Amen.

At fixed hours

The Angelus

Established Catholic prayer

The Angel of the Lord declared unto Mary.
And she conceived of the Holy Spirit.

Hail Mary…

Behold the handmaid of the Lord.
Be it done unto me according to thy word.

Hail Mary…

And the Word was made flesh.
And dwelt among us.

Hail Mary…

Pray for us, O holy Mother of God.
That we may be made worthy of the promises of Christ.

Let us pray. Pour forth, we beseech thee, O Lord, thy grace into our hearts; that we, to whom the Incarnation of Christ thy Son was made known by the message of an angel, may by his Passion and Cross be brought to the glory of his Resurrection. Through the same Christ our Lord. Amen.

Repentance

Act of Contrition

Established Catholic form

O my God, I am heartily sorry for having offended you, and I detest all my sins because of your just punishments, but most of all because they offend you, my God, who are all good and deserving of all my love. I firmly resolve, with the help of your grace, to sin no more and to avoid the near occasions of sin. Amen.

Discouragement or temptation

Jesus Prayer

Ancient Christian prayer

Lord Jesus Christ, Son of God, have mercy on me, a sinner.

Brief prayer after a fall

Original course prayer

Jesus, I trust in You. Have mercy on me, forgive me, and help me begin again without delay.

For protection and confidence

Memorare

Established Catholic prayer

Remember, O most gracious Virgin Mary, that never was it known that anyone who fled to thy protection, implored thy help, or sought thy intercession was left unaided. Inspired by this confidence, I fly unto thee, O Virgin of virgins, my Mother. To thee do I come, before thee I stand, sinful and sorrowful. O Mother of the Word Incarnate, despise not my petitions, but in thy mercy hear and answer me. Amen.

At night

Into your hands

Traditional scriptural response

Into your hands, O Lord, I commend my spirit.

Course night prayer

Original to this course

Lord, receive what was completed, what failed and what remains unfinished. Grant me pardon, rest and grace to begin again tomorrow. Amen.

Text note: established Catholic prayers can vary slightly between approved prayer books, translations and local usage. A published version should retain one consistent approved form and clearly preserve the distinction between traditional texts and prayers written for this course.
