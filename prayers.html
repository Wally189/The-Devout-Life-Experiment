Prayers for the work

Established Catholic prayers are identified as such. The short lesson prayer is original to this course.

Before study

Original prayer

Lord Jesus Christ, give me light to see what is true, humility to accept it, courage to amend what is wrong, and peace to begin again when I fall. Let this work lead not to self-absorption but to love of You and faithful service of others. Amen.

Come, Holy Spirit

Come, Holy Spirit, fill the hearts of your faithful and kindle in them the fire of your love. Send forth your Spirit and they shall be created, and you shall renew the face of the earth.

O God, who by the light of the Holy Spirit did instruct the hearts of the faithful, grant that by the same Holy Spirit we may be truly wise and ever rejoice in his consolations. Through Christ our Lord. Amen.

Act of Contrition

O my God, I am heartily sorry for having offended you, and I detest all my sins because of your just punishments, but most of all because they offend you, my God, who are all good and deserving of all my love. I firmly resolve, with the help of your grace, to sin no more and to avoid the near occasions of sin. Amen.

Jesus Prayer

Lord Jesus Christ, Son of God, have mercy on me, a sinner.

Morning Offering

O Jesus, through the Immaculate Heart of Mary, I offer you my prayers, works, joys and sufferings of this day for all the intentions of your Sacred Heart, in union with the Holy Sacrifice of the Mass throughout the world, for the salvation of souls, the reparation of sins, the reunion of all Christians, and in particular for the intentions of the Holy Father this month. Amen.

After a fall

Brief traditional form

Jesus, I trust in You. Have mercy on me, forgive me, and help me begin again without delay.

Texts can vary slightly between approved Catholic prayer books and local usage. Before publication, compare the chosen wording with a recognised Catholic prayer book or diocesan source.
