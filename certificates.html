Completion framework

Certificates

The certificate framework has not yet been released. Any future record will describe the exact course block completed and the evidence used.

Evidence of study

Completion will depend on genuine reading, prayer, handwritten review and proportionate practical amendment—not merely opening pages.

Clear limits

No certificate will claim academic accreditation, professional qualification, medical benefit or ecclesiastical approval.

Honest scope

The final wording and completion test will be approved before any certificate is made available.
