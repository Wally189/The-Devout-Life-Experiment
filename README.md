# The Devout Life Experiment

A private Catholic experiment in self-knowledge, amendment of life, prayer and practical care of the whole person.

Built around:
- St Francis de Sales, *Introduction to the Devout Life*
- Lorenzo Scupoli, *The Spiritual Combat*
- R. P. Quadrupani, *Light and Peace*
- the Catechism of the Catholic Church
- the sacramental and penitential life of the Church

This is a personal study resource, not medical treatment, psychotherapy, spiritual direction or a substitute for pastoral or professional help.

## Structure

The suggested course lasts at least twelve weeks. Each lesson follows the same method:

**Prepare → Read → Recall → Understand → Examine → Distinguish → Amend → Practise → Pray → Review → Redirect**

The site is static and suitable for GitHub Pages. The contact form is deliberately left in preparation mode until a Formspree endpoint is supplied.
