# The Devout and Practical Life Experiment

A Catholic-inspired experiment in spiritual formation, amendment of life and practical stability for laypeople.

Built principally around:

- St Francis de Sales, *Introduction to the Devout Life*
- Lorenzo Scupoli, *The Spiritual Combat*
- R. P. Quadrupani, *Light and Peace*
- the Catechism of the Catholic Church
- Catholic prayer and sacramental life

This is a personal study resource, not medical treatment, psychotherapy, spiritual direction or a substitute for pastoral or professional help.

## Structure

The first course cycle contains 26 gentle weeks. Each week begins on Sunday and normally asks for about 20–30 minutes a day. Readers may follow:

- the spiritual reading track;
- the practical stability track;
- or both together through one proportionate amendment.

The weekly rhythm is:

**Orient → Read → Recall → Compare → Examine → Amend → Practise and Review**

The course repeatedly permits a slower pace, repeated weeks and a reduced minimum during illness or disruption. Its central recovery instruction is: **Do not restart. Resume.**

## Technical notes

The site is static and deployed through GitHub Pages. `app.js` only initialises the shared namespace; the current navigation, course data and rendering functions live in `site.js`.

The contact form uses the shared Formspree endpoint and is limited to corrections, broken links, source suggestions and general feedback.
