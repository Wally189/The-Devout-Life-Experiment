Books and materials

The minimum is the principal book, one ruled notebook, a pen or pencil, prayer and enough honesty to write plainly. Free online texts are provided; printed editions and coloured pens are optional.

The notebook

  • Use the front for spiritual lessons and reviews.
  • Start from the back for the Practical Register, action pages and plans.
  • Number pages as the notebook develops.
  • Use a pencil for provisional material.
  • Use different ink colours only where they make review easier.

Ordinary kit

  • One ruled A4 or A5 notebook
  • One reliable black or blue pen
  • A pencil and eraser
  • Optional red and green pens for warnings and completed actions
  • A calendar for settled study and practical actions
  • Bookmarks or slips for assigned chapters
  • Parish, GP or appropriate support details when a matter requires them
Edition note: chapter numbering and wording may differ slightly between translations. Use the lesson’s part and chapter references, and check the contents page of your edition before beginning. Do not delay starting merely to collect an ideal set of books or stationery.
