The notebook

  • Leave the first two pages for an index.
  • Use the front for spiritual lessons and reviews.
  • Start from the back for the Practical Register, action pages and plans.
  • Number pages and date entries.
  • Use pencil or colour only where it makes later review easier.

Ordinary kit

  • One ruled A4 or A5 notebook
  • One reliable black or blue pen
  • A pencil and eraser
  • Optional additional pens for actions, dates or completed items
  • A calendar for study sessions and practical actions
  • Bookmarks or paper slips for assigned chapters
  • Parish, GP and appropriate specialist support details where needed
Begin with the minimum. Preparation can become avoidance. Week 1 requires only access to St Francis de Sales, a notebook and a pen.
