Books and materials

The minimum is the principal book, one notebook, a pen or pencil, prayer and enough honesty to write plainly. Free online texts are provided; printed editions are optional.

Principal text

Introduction to the Devout Life

St Francis de Sales provides the course spine: purification, prayer, virtue, temptation, ordinary duties and renewal.

Ordinary kit

  • One ruled notebook reserved for the experiment
  • One reliable pen and a pencil for the reading method
  • A calendar for settled study and practical actions
  • Bookmarks or slips for assigned chapters
  • Parish, GP or appropriate support details when a matter requires them
Edition note: chapter numbering and wording may differ slightly between translations. Use the lesson’s part and chapter references, and check the contents page of your edition before beginning.
