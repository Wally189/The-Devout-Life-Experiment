Materials

Core books

  • St Francis de Sales, Introduction to the Devout Life — principal course spine.
  • Lorenzo Scupoli, The Spiritual Combat — practical treatment of passions, temptation and perseverance.
  • R. P. Quadrupani, Light and Peace — safeguard against agitation, harshness and discouragement.
  • Catechism of the Catholic Church — doctrinal reference.
  • Code of Canon Law, especially canons 1249–1253 — penitential days, fasting and abstinence.

Ordinary tools

  • One paper notebook reserved for the experiment.
  • A reliable pen and pencil.
  • Printed lesson or journal pages where helpful.
  • A calendar for the weekly study rhythm.
  • A simple envelope, account or budgeting method where money is the amendment.
  • Contact details for parish, GP or appropriate support services when needed.

How to use editions responsibly

The original works are long out of copyright, but modern translations, introductions and layouts may remain protected. For private study, use any lawful edition you own or can access. Before public publication, use a clearly public-domain edition, obtain permission, or write original summaries with attribution.

Do not collect equipment as a substitute for beginning. The complete minimum is one book, paper, pencil, prayer and an honest hour.
