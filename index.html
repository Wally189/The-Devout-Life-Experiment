A private Catholic experiment in amendment of life

Welcome to The Devout Life Experiment.

A twelve-week reading journey through St Francis de Sales’ Introduction to the Devout Life, supported by The Spiritual Combat, Light and Peace, prayer, the sacraments, handwriting and honest reflection.

Begin the experiment →

The recurring method

Read. Recall. Understand. Examine. Amend. Practise. Pray. Review. Redirect.

The subject changes each week. The method remains deliberately simple.

Lessons

Open the twelve book-led lessons and complete the guided journal at the end of each one.

Schedule

Follow the suggested twelve-week course, or slow it down where a lesson needs longer.

Materials

Open lawful online editions of the three books and the official Church references.

Journal

Use the same guided reading, examination, amendment and review framework each week.

Prayers

Verified Catholic prayers for study, repentance, perseverance and peace.

Completion

A simple record that the lessons were completed—not a professional qualification.

Important: this is Catholic spiritual study and self-care, not a replacement for confession, spiritual direction, medical care, counselling or addiction support.
