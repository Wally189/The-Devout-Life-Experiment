Twelve weeks, then begin again

Not self-optimisation. Amendment of life.

This private experiment asks whether sustained reading of Catholic spiritual tradition—joined to prayer, the sacraments, honest self-examination, appropriate healthcare and one concrete amendment at a time—can reorder an ordinary life towards God.

The recurring method

Prepare → Read → Recall → Understand → Examine → Distinguish → Amend → Practise → Pray → Review → Redirect.

The repeated method is deliberate. The subject changes; the work remains stable.

Book-led

St Francis de Sales provides the spine, with Scupoli, Quadrupani, the Catechism and the Church’s penitential discipline as companions.

Sacramental

Prayer, Mass, confession and pastoral counsel are not decorative additions. They are part of the work.

Whole-person care

Sin is not confused with illness; healthcare is not confused with absolution. Spiritual, practical and clinical means each have their proper place.

Important: fasting, alcohol reduction, smoking cessation, severe low mood, trauma, dependency and other health matters may require medical or specialist advice. Do not use this course to delay necessary care.
