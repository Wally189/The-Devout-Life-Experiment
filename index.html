A Catholic-inspired experiment in amendment of life

See life truthfully. Repair the next thing. Begin again gently.

A twenty-seven-week course in spiritual formation and ordinary duty, guided principally by St Francis de Sales, with Lorenzo Scupoli, R. P. Quadrupani, the Catechism, Catholic prayer and sacramental life.

You may use it three ways

Spiritual only: follow the weekly reading and handwritten review.

Practical only: use the register, topic exercises, task method and gradual Rule of Life.

Together: let each week’s reading inform one practical amendment.

Start here

Today, do only this

  1. Find one ordinary notebook and pen.
  2. Open Week 1.
  3. Read Sunday’s orientation.
  4. Stop. The rest belongs to later days.

Normal daily time: about 20–30 minutes across spiritual and practical administration. Practical actions themselves may take longer. A slower pace, repeated week or single-track period is permitted.

27 gentle weeks

St Francis de Sales is read in smaller portions, beginning on Sunday and ending with Saturday review.

Practical stability

Keep a register, choose one next action, use focused topic exercises and discern a provisional lay Rule of Life.

How to use it

Choose spiritual, practical or combined use. Follow the ordinary week or the minimum faithful version.

When life goes wrong

Use a reduced rule for illness, crisis, disruption, discouragement or a week that simply collapses.
