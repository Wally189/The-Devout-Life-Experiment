Welcome to The Devout and Practical Life Experiment.

A guided plan for reading, prayer, discernment and ordinary action. St Francis de Sales’ Introduction to the Devout Life provides the main course; Lorenzo Scupoli’s The Spiritual Combat, R. P. Quadrupani’s Light and Peace, the Catechism, Catholic prayer and sacramental life provide carefully chosen support.

Two connected forms of work

The lessons form judgement through ordered spiritual reading, prayer and one weekly amendment.

The practical method helps identify real duties and problems, record next actions, review evidence and gradually discern a sustainable lay Rule of Life.

Lessons

Follow twelve book-led weeks from Monday reading to Sunday handwritten review.

Practical

Keep a practical register, action pages and reviews while discerning—not rushing—a lay Rule of Life.
